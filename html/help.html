About Jack's Mails

What is Jack's Mails ?

Jack's Mails is a web browser extension that enables the creation and use of temporary email addresses. Jack's Mails also provides a password management service.

Temporary emails

Email addresses are managed by mail.tm, and Jack's Mails utilizes the mail.tm API for the creation, management, and utilization of email addresses.
You can create and manage temporary email addresses, with a maximum limit of 10 addresses. While your address itself is not temporary, the messages received are automatically deleted after 7 days. Our service allows you to receive messages, but sending messages is not supported. Usage of Jack's Mails for illegal activity is strictly prohibited.

Two methods are available to create an email address

  • Create an address by specifying the desired name
    For the name field, do not specify the domain name (@example.com).
    The password is useful only for logging in from a service other than Jack's Mails. Jack's Mails stores your credentials so you don't have to memorize them to access your mailbox.

  • Create an address with a random name
    You can't specify both the name and the password for the address. If you want to log in from another service, you must not use this method.

Passwords manager

You can save your passwords and usernames along with their associated URLs. You can use the search bar to quickly find a password for a specific URL.
All your data is stored locally on your own computer.

Settings

  • Change your password
    Change your primary password, as it grants you access to the application. Please be cautious, as forgetting it will make it impossible to recover.

  • Change the connection duration
    Specify the duration of inactivity after which you will be automatically logged out.

What about security and privacy ?

User security and privacy are at the core of our concerns.

  • Privacy
    All your login information is stored locally on your own computer, ensuring that only you have access to this data.
    Jack's Mails does not collect any user-related data.
    As for email accounts, they are managed by mail.tm and are subject to their privacy policy.

  • Security
    All your email account login information and passwords are stored encrypted using the AES-128 algorithm. The encryption key is not stored but is generated when the user logs in and deleted when he logs out. The master password (used to access the application) is stored hashed with the SHA-512 algorithm.
    We have also implemented several security mechanisms to ensure the application's resilience against malicious user intrusions.
